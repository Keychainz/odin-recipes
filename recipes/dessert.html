Instant Ramen Noodles

The most authentic Ramen Noodles

So this one has a lot of ingredients so lets begin. You'll need:

  • Instant Ramen Pack
  • Water
  1. Boil the water, then add in the seasoning mix. The instructions in the package says to add the noodles first. Thats wrong.
  2. Then add the noodles in and only cook them for like 3 minutes, any longer and you go past that al dente noodle.
  3. Now plate it and serve it. Enjoy!
