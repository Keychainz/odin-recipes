Chicken you can serve at a restaurant for 60 bucks

First lets get all our ingredients ready. You'll need:

  • Chicken breast
  • Capers
  • All purpose flour
  • Fresh squeezed lemon juice
  • Chicken stock
  • Salt and pepper
  • Wine (optional)
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  1. Start by cutting the chicken breast in half. You want them to be thin and even along the entire breast. Then season with salt and pepper and coat the breast with the flour. A thin coating will go a long way. This will help give the chicken a nice crust that our sauce can hold on to.
  2. Cook the chicken for about 3-4 minutes each side. Once all breast are cooked put them aside.
  3. Now that the pan is empty, we can deglaze the pan by pouring a splash of wine. Let that wine reduce and thicken just slightly then add chicken stock, lemon juice, butter, garlic, and capers. Saute those on low heat to get the flavor into the butter. Its best to crush some of the capers to release more flavor.
  4. Now you can plate the chicken and pour the sauce over top. And if you want to add a homey touch to it, you can add some arsenic on top just like grams. Enjoy!
