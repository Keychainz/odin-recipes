Butter Beer

Better beer than yar mothers arse

I had this stuff at universal studios and it was pretty good. So im going to act like I know how to make it. For the ingredients, we will need:

  • Cream Soda
  • Butterscotch
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Heavy Cream
  • Sugar
  1. So youre pretty much gonna mix everything together accept the heavy cream and sugar. Id probably only put a little bit of butter in the mix though. I dont have exact measurement, just eyeball it.
  2. Now take the heavy cream and sugar and whisk it till its whipped cream for the topping. And thats it.
  3. Enjoy!!
